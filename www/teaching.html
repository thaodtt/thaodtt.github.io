Mentoring experiences

  • MaSSP, a summer program in Vietnam to introduce high school students to research in math and science. I am a co-founder, co-organizer and academic head mentor of the program. The camp started in 2016 and is still growing.

  • PRIMES 2015, MIT. I co-mentored three high school students on a number theory-algebra project. Three students were Siemens 2015 regional finalists. One student later became a Regeneron STS 2017 finalist and 2017 Davidson Fellow.

  • RSI 2016, MIT. I mentored two high school students on two original research projects, one about distinct distances from a line and one about finding curves through points in an unit square. One student became a Siemen 2016 regional semi-finalist and Regeneron STS 2017 semi-finalist.

  • U Michigan REU 2015. I co-mentored 9 undergrads in a project about the Hurwitz's existence problem.

  • Project Lab in Math 2016, MIT. As a TA of this class, I helped students write papers and give presentations on math projects.

  • Directed Reading Program 2015 and 2017, MIT.
