Math and Science Summer Program (MASSP) is a program for high school students in Vietnam.The aim is to introduce the students to research and applications of math and computer science. This is sponsored by MIT VSA and Hanoi Education University.

Parcitipating in this program, the students will have the opportunity to conduct math research, build CS applications, develope team work skills and presentation skills.
